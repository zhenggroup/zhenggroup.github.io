Our review paper is accepted by Chemistry - An Asian Journal (Wiley)

In the review, we presented the current development of electrochemical scanning tunneling microscope for electrocatalysis studies.
Title: Observing Electrocatalytic Processes via In Situ Electrochemical Scanning Tunneling Microscopy: Latest Advances
link: https://doi.org/10.1002/asia.202200384

Our review paper is accepted by Matter (Cell press)

In the review, we analysed the mass production methods of 2D materials via exfoliation from 6 aspects, providing an overview for the 2D material industry.
Title: Beyond Sonication: Advanced Exfoliation Methods for Scalable Production of 2D Materials
link: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.matt.2021.12.010
