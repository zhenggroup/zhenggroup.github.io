Bio

Dr. Weiran Zheng received his B.Sc. degree in Chemistry from Wuhan University in 2009 and was awarded a Chinese Scholarship Council (CSC) joint Ph.D. scholarship in 2012 to study at the University of Oxford. After receiving his Ph.D. in Physical Chemistry in late 2015, he worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow (later Research Fellow) at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He joined Guangdong Technion - Israel Institute of Technology (GTIIT) in January 2022 as a tenure-track associate professor.
