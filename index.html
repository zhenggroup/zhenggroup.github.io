The Zheng Group

Electrochemical Interface & Conversion Lab @ GTIIT

Welcome to our research group. We are dedicated to advancing the understanding of electrochemical interfaces and conversion processes. Our work leverages state-of-the-art spectroelectrochemistry and scanning probe microscopy techniques to investigate fundamental challenges in electrocatalysis. Key areas of our research include elucidating the mechanisms of electrocatalyst deactivation, tracking the dynamic evolution of surface sites during reactions, and developing and applying advanced electrochemical methods.
