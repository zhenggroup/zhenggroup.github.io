Welcome to

The Electrochemical Interface and Conversion Lab

at Guangdong Technion-Israel Institute of Technology (GTIIT)

广东以色列理工学院 | 电化学界面与转化研究组 | 郑蔚然副教授

The group is led by Dr. Weiran Zheng. We focus on understanding electrochemical processes using in situ techniques, including morphologic and spectroscopic methods. We want to correlate electrochemical signals (potential, current, and resistance) with the physical evolution of electrodes and the interfaces, providing insights into rational electrode design that can solve real problems in current electrocatalysis.
