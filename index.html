We are the
Electrochemical Interface and Conversion (EIC) Group
at Guangdong Technion (GTIIT)
广东以色列理工学院电化学界面与转化研究组

The EIC research group is led by Dr. Weiran Zheng, currently an Assistant Professor of Chemistry in GTIIT.
***
We are interested in electrochemically charged interface and *in situ* characterization techniques for electrochemical conversion processes, such as water splitting reaction and carbon dioxide reduction. We aim to understand and solve the real-life problems in electrochemical conversion from an atomic/molecular level.
