Our lab focuses on understanding electrochemical processes using in situ techniques, including morphologic and spectroscopic methods. We want to correlate electrochemical signals (potential, current, and resistance) with the physical evolution of electrodes and the interfaces, providing insights into rational electrode design that can solve real problems in current electrocatalysis.
