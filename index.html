The Zheng Group

Electrochemical Interface & Conversion Lab @ GTIIT

Led by Dr. Weiran Zheng 郑蔚然, we study electrochemical interfaces in real time with operando spectroscopy, scanning probe microscopy, and data-driven electrochemical methods. Our goal is to reveal how catalytic activity, selectivity, and degradation evolve under working conditions, enabling more efficient and durable electrocatalysis.
