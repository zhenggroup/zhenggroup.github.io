The Electrochemical Interface and Conversion Lab

at Guangdong Technion-Israel Institute of Technology (GTIIT)

电化学界面与转化实验室
广东以色列理工学院

Lab PI
Weiran Zheng, Associate Professor of Chemistry

We focus on understanding electrochemical processes using in situ techniques, including morphologic and spectroscopic methods.
We want to correlate electrochemical signals (potential, current, and resistance) with the physical evolution of electrodes and the interfaces, providing insights into rational electrode design that can solve real problems in current electrocatalysis.
