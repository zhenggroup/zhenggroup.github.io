Welcome to the
Electrochemical Interface and Conversion Group
at Guangdong Technion

We develop electrocatalytic systems for high atomic efficiency

We focus on atomic understanding of  electrochemical interface

PreviousNext

Group leader: Dr. Weiran ZHENG
Assistant Professor of Chemistry at Guangdong Technion - Israel Institute of Technology (GTIIT)

Electrochemistry represents a significant aspect of Modern Physical Chemistry and is the art of using the potential to play electrons in a well-controlled fashion. Our group focuses on three major topics:
a. Understanding electrochemical interface at atomic level using in situ techniques coupled with electrochemistry;
b. Rational designing of active sites for high atomic efficiency;
c. Developing integrated electrocatalytic systems for all-in-one treatment and conversion.
