##### Postdoc Researchers/Research Scientists

###### Description
Currently, we have **1** research scientist/postdoc position available in the group. The project will be related to in situ analysis of CO2 electrochemical reduction and transport using Raman and AFM-SECM. Other electrocatalysis processes, such as seawater splitting, will also be involved. We also welcome other related topics proposed by the candidate.

###### Requirement

- electrochemical corrosion of metal and metal oxides
